Collaborate with us

Our project is a collaborative community effort. We look for collaborators willing to contribute and adapt together their own data, be it a single text, a lexical study or a text corpus to our data format. Each contributor will be credited in the iClassifier reports wherever their data appears and will have control over the publication of their data. Join us in creating together a comperhensive image of ancient lexicons and their inner organizations!

Contributors recieve credentials for our digital input forms, allowing them to conduct philological study, add images per example and per text, and connect their data to the TLA guidelines (for ancient Egyptian), to standardized metadata annotations (https://thot.philo.ulg.ac.be/), or to the relevant corpus projects and ontologies in their academic discipline.
