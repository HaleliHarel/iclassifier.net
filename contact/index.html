Collaborate with us

Our project is a collaborative, community-driven effort.
We are looking for collaborators who are willing to contribute and annotate their own data using our research tool.
Contributions can range from a single text to a lexical study or even an entire text corpus.
Each contributor will be credited in the iClassifier reports wherever their data appears and will retain control over the publication of their data.
Join us in building a comprehensive picture of ancient lexicons and their internal organization!
