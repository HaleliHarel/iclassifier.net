Collaborate with us

Our project is a collaborative community effort. We look for collaborators willing to adapt their own data,
be it a single text, a lexical study or a text corpus to our data format. Each contributor will be credited in the iClassifier reports wherever their data appears. Join us in creating together a comperhensive image of ancient lexicons and their inner organizations!

Contributors recieve credentials for our digital input forms, allowing them to conduct philological study, add images per example and per text, and connect their data to the TLA guidelines (for ancient Egyptian) or to the relevant corpus projects in each researched discipline.
